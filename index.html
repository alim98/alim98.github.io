About Me

Building the future of computational intelligence

I'm an AI researcher based in Frankfurt, Germany, currently working as a Research Assistant at the Max Planck Institute for Brain Research under Prof. Gilles Laurent. My work sits at the crossroads of AI and neuroscience—designing learning systems inspired by the brain.

My research focuses on representation learning, spiking neural networks, and medical image analysis. I hold an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence & Robotics from the University of Tehran and I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of what's possible with deep learning.

 Architectural Concept

                        

                            The foundation model is a custom 3D MAE. Operating on 40x40x40 input crops with a patch size of 2, it processes local ultrastructure. By randomly masking
                            75% of the patches, the network is forced to
                            understand the deep geometric and contextual relationships of vesicle membranes rather than
                            relying on local interpolation.
                        

                        

                            Trained on 20,000 unlabeled vesicle-centered crops, the 12-layer ViT encoder learns a
                            powerful representation prior. The Decoder
                            injects learnable [MASK] tokens to restore the sequence length, predicting the
                            original raw EM. Once trained, the Decoder is discarded and the Encoder acts as the
                            segmentation backbone.
